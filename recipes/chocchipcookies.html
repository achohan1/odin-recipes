Chocolate Chip Cookies

Quick and delicious!!

Ingredients

  • 158g butter softened
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 218g all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 100g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. mix sugar and butter until fully combined
  2. add egg and vanilla extract, mix until combined
  3. scrape down sides of the mixing bowl then add in flour and baking powder and mix until smooth
  4. add chocolate chips and mix until evenly distributed
  5. heat oven to 180 degrees
  6. line baking tray and portion chocolate chip cookies and place on tray
  7. place tray in oven and bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown
  8. leave to cool and enjoy
